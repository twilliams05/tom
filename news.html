GTA VI confirmed as being in 'early development' by Rockstar Games

Rockstar Games' next game is in early development and fans can't wait to find out more. It is expected to be released in 2021/2022, a possible 8 or 9 years after the release of GTA V. Star Wars Battlefront 2 To Release One Last Update

Star Wars Battlefront is set to recieve a final update in the near future which has left some people sad to see such a great game come to an end. We don’t really know what’s in store for the patch for now, but there have been several bugs online that have been pointed out that still need attention. A lot of Vader mains say that he needs to get worked on after the latest patch, and some think that DICE should definitely lower the kill requirements for Darth Maul’s Old Master skin.
